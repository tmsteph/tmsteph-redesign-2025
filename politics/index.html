Preamble

Recognizing the inherent dignity and equal rights of every person, this Bill affirms protections and freedoms essential to human flourishing, collective well-being, and sustainable co-existence.

Core Articles

Equality & Dignity

Rights apply to everyone without discrimination on any basis. No right may be denied or diluted because of identity, belief, or background.

Housing

Everyone has the right to safe, secure, and adequate housing with privacy, sanitation, and protection from hazards. Homelessness must be eliminated through fair, measurable action.

Health

Everyone deserves the highest attainable standard of physical and mental health, with access to preventive care, essential medicines, and respectful public health measures.

Nutrition

Everyone has the right to adequate, nutritious, and culturally appropriate food, secured through sustainable systems and fair distribution.

Freedom of Movement

People may move freely within their country and travel across borders via fair, transparent, and non-discriminatory processes.

Privacy & Expression

Privacy, data protection, free expression, and peaceful assembly are safeguarded, with limits only when lawful, necessary, and proportionate.

Implementation & Accountability

Rights are universal, interdependent, and indivisible. Laws, policies, and remedies should make these rights real, ensure transparency, and invite public participation with independent oversight.

Individuals share a duty to respect one another and uphold equality, dignity, and justice.
